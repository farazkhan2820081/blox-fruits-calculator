FAQ

How do I level up quickly in Blox Fruits?

To level up quickly in Blox Fruits, focus on completing high XP quests, use AoE fruits for efficient farming, and allocate your stat points wisely between **Defense**, **Melee**, and **Fruit**.

What are the best fruits for grinding in Blox Fruits?

The best fruits for grinding are **Magma**, **Light**, and **Venom**. These fruits have powerful AoE abilities that help clear large groups of enemies quickly.
