Fruit Spawning Mechanics

Spawn Timer

Fruits spawn every 45 minutes to 1 hour and 15 minutes on random islands. Only one fruit can spawn per server at a time.

Despawn Timer

Fruits despawn after 20 minutes if not picked up. The timer resets when a new fruit spawns.

Rarity System

Higher rarity fruits have lower spawn chances. Legendary fruits are extremely rare compared to common ones.
