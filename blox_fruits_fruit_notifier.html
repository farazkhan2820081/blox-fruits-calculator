FAQ

How does the Fruit Notifier work?

The Fruit Notifier lets you select a fruit and set a timer for when it spawns in the game. You will receive a notification when the fruit appears.

Can I receive notifications for all fruits?

Currently, the notifier allows you to select a single fruit to monitor at a time. We plan to add multi-fruit monitoring in future updates.
