Tier List Explanation

S Tier - Overpowered

These fruits dominate both PvP and PvE content. They offer exceptional damage, utility, and survivability. Perfect for competitive play and efficient grinding.

A Tier - Excellent

Strong fruits that excel in specific areas. Great for most content with good damage output and useful abilities. Solid choices for both casual and competitive players.

B Tier - Good

Decent fruits with some strengths but also notable weaknesses. Can be effective in the right hands but may struggle against higher-tier options.

C Tier - Average

Fruits that are functional but outclassed by higher-tier options. May have niche uses but generally not recommended for competitive play.
